The Threat

Plans are currently being drawn up to demolish Egleston Hall and build a replacement.

This decision would waste tens of millions of dollars. Demolition costs, new construction, and replacing this level of craftsmanship would far exceed the cost of preserving and renovating what we already have.

Once this 1918 building is gone, we'll have spent a fortune to lose something priceless. There are smarter, more economical solutions that preserve this building and serve our community's needs.

Smart Preservation, Not Costly Demolition

Renovation and preservation make economic sense. Join our campaign to explore cost-effective alternatives that save both money and this historic cornerstone of our community.
